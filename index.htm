MASK MATCH





RULES! ...are meant to be broken. (but not these ones!)

Player one takes the first turn.

During player's turn, player flips two cards.

If player uncovers a matching pair of cards, player is awarded a point and takes another turn.

If no match is made, it is Player Two's turn.

Player with the most points after all cards are matched is the winner.

If score is equal, it is a tie.
